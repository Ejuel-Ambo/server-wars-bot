================================================================================
SERVER WARS: DOMINION PROTOCOL - PRIZE & INCENTIVE SYSTEM
================================================================================

This document redesigns the prize and incentive system to be sustainable,
non-pay-to-win, and focused on retention and engagement.

================================================================================
1. DESIGN PRINCIPLES
================================================================================

CORE PRINCIPLES:

1. NON-PAY-TO-WIN
   - Prizes never give gameplay advantages
   - Focus on status, recognition, and cosmetics
   - Free players can compete equally
   - Premium is about prestige, not power

2. SERVER-FOCUSED REWARDS
   - Rewards benefit the entire server community
   - Encourage group coordination
   - Shared victory feels more meaningful
   - Individual contributions recognized within team context

3. SUSTAINABLE MODEL
   - No large cash prizes (unsustainable for indie dev)
   - Digital rewards with low/no cost
   - Partnership opportunities for scaling
   - Revenue-neutral or positive

4. RETENTION-DRIVEN
   - Rewards encourage continued play
   - Long-term engagement incentivized
   - Season-to-season progression
   - Multiple reward tiers (not just top 3)

5. MULTIPLE PATHS TO VICTORY
   - Not just military dominance
   - Economic achievement
   - Diplomatic mastery
   - Consistent participation
   - Community contribution

================================================================================
2. REWARD CATEGORIES
================================================================================

CATEGORY A: STATUS & RECOGNITION

- Unique borders on embeds

Impact: Quality of life, not power

----------------------------

- Exclusive Discord server access
- Bot premium features

CATEGORY E: COMMUNITY RECOGNITION
----------------------------------
Cost: Free
Impact: Social value
Examples:
- Featured in bot status
- Twitter/social media shoutout
- Developer interview/spotlight
- Community video feature
- Official lore inclusion
- Named events or seasons

7. PRIZE POOL FUNDING STRATEGY
================================================================================

SUSTAINABLE FUNDING:

Phase 1 (MVP - Phase 2-3): NO PRIZES
- Focus on gameplay and retention
- Build community first
- Test systems thoroughly
- No financial burden on developer

Phase 2 (Seasons Launch - Phase 4): MINIMAL PRIZES
- Status and cosmetic rewards only
- Developer provides premium features as prizes
- Cost: Time only, no cash
- Build prize culture

Phase 3 (Post-Monetization - Phase 5+): SCALED PRIZES
- Use 20% of premium subscription revenue for prizes
- Discord Nitro partnerships (reach out to Discord)
- Gaming platform partnerships (Steam, Epic, etc.)
- Sponsor small prize pools
- Community donation option (optional)

Example Revenue Math:
- 100 servers playing
- 3% conversion = 3 premium servers
- Average $15/month = $45/month revenue
- 20% to prizes = $9/month prize pool
- Sustainable, but small initially

GROWTH MODEL:
As bot grows to 1000+ servers:
- 30 premium servers Ã— $15 = $450/month
- 20% to prizes = $90/month
- Can offer Discord Nitro to top 3 winners
- Plus status rewards for top 25%

PARTNERSHIP OPPORTUNITIES:
- Approach Discord for Nitro partnership
- Gaming companies for game key prizes
- Server hosting for free hosting prizes
- Gaming peripheral companies
- Indie game developers

COMMUNITY CONTRIBUTION (OPTIONAL):
- Allow servers to contribute to prize pool
- Fully transparent accounting
- Vote on prize distribution
- Optional, never required

================================================================================
8. PRIZE DISTRIBUTION MECHANICS
================================================================================

CLAIMING PRIZES:
1. Season ends, rankings finalized
2. Winners announced in all servers
3. Winners DM bot with /claim_prize command
4. Bot verifies eligibility
5. Prize code or access granted
6. Tracking for accountability

VERIFICATION:
- Check for cheating/abuse before awarding
- Review flagged accounts
- Confirm active participation
- Prevent multi-accounting

PRIZE DELIVERY:
Digital Prizes:
- Discord Nitro: Direct bot delivery or code
- Gift cards: Emailed code or DM
- Premium features: Automatic activation
- Badges/titles: Automatic grant

Status Rewards:
- Automatic title grant
- Badge application
- Hall of Fame update
- Announcements sent

UNCLAIMED PRIZES:
- 30-day claim window
- After 30 days, prize forfeited
- Can be rolled into next season
- Prevents abuse

================================================================================
9. ANTI-ABUSE MEASURES
================================================================================

PREVENT CHEATING:

Multi-Accounting:
- Track IP addresses (basic)
- Unusual patterns detection
- Cross-reference Discord accounts
- Ban all accounts if caught

Collusion:
- Monitor alliance patterns
- Check for arranged outcomes
- Community reporting
- Review suspicious activity

Bot/Automation:
- Rate limiting
- CAPTCHA for prize claims (if needed)
- Unusual activity detection
- Manual review of winners

Gaming the System:
- Hidden MMR/ranking factors
- Not purely based on visible stats
- Qualitative factors considered
- Developer oversight

DISQUALIFICATION RULES:
- Cheating: Immediate disqualification, ban
- Multiple accounts: All accounts disqualified
- Abuse/harassment: Disqualification + ban
- Collusion: Both parties disqualified
- Bot usage: Disqualification + ban

CONCLUSION
================================================================================

CORE STRATEGY:
1. Start with status rewards (free)
2. Build engaged community
3. Add monetization (optional for players)
4. Use 20% of revenue for prizes
5. Seek partnerships for scaling
6. Always maintain non-pay-to-win

This creates a sustainable, fair, and exciting reward system that:
- Doesn't burden the solo developer financially
- Maintains competitive integrity
- Rewards skill and dedication
- Scales with the bot's success
- Keeps players engaged long-term

Focus on making winning FEEL amazing through recognition and status,
not just monetary value. The community's respect and historical recognition
can be more motivating than small cash prizes.
